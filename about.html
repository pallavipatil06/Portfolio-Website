About

My Story

"Hi, I am Pallavi! I am a UI/UX designer based in Austin, with a background in architecture and passion for cooking. I gained my bachelors of Architecture from University of Mumbai in 2002. After working as Architect in India for 7 years, I came to the USA. In Long Island, NY I started working as Assistant Project manager at one of the contracting companies for the next two years. I took a break from working to start a family. To get back into my career I decided to upgrade my skills in a field by doing a UI designer course at CareerFoundry. With a background in architecture I have experience in designing, detailing and sensitivity to user needs which helped while learning UI designing. As a designer, my main aim is to create an aesthetically appealing User Interface which is functional and easy to use. Currently I am continuing my education with Front End development, reading and updating myself with design innovations. "
